About Us

CDS is a company based upon the pursuit of innovation. Our goal is to push the automotive industry to new heights never before thought possible.

Our history

CDS was started as a kickstarter in 2013, and very quickly reached its goal of 3 million dollars after 600,000 anonymous users donated exactly 5 dollars each to us. Afterwards, we were bought out by a Brazillian company called Mischievous Motors, a very reputable car dealership. We knew from the start our first product would be the CD-1, and have been working non-stop for 11 years on fufilling that goal. In 2016 our first working prototype was produced, but it still had a long way to go. Now, in 2024, the result of all our work, the CD-1, is nearly ready to release.
